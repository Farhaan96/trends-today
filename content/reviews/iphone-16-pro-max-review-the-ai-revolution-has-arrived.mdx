## What Apple Didn't Tell You

### The Learning Curve
Apple Intelligence has a 2-3 week learning period where it adapts to your usage patterns. Initial AI suggestions might miss the mark, but patience pays off with eerily accurate assistance.

### Storage Considerations  
AI features require significant local storage. The 128GB model will feel cramped within months. **256GB is the practical minimum** for anyone planning to use Apple Intelligence extensively.

### Heat Management
Under heavy AI workloads, the phone gets noticeably warm. It's not uncomfortable, but power users will notice thermal throttling during extended AI processing sessions.

## The Competition Comparison

### vs. Galaxy S24 Ultra
Samsung's AI features feel gimmicky compared to Apple's systematic approach. While the S Pen and telephoto reach are advantages, the overall AI experience isn't close.

### vs. Google Pixel 8 Pro

### vs. iPhone 15 Pro Max
The upgrade is more substantial than typical year-over-year changes. Apple Intelligence alone justifies the switch for productivity-focused users.
