# Google's Willow Chip Just Solved a 10,000-Year Problem in 5 Minutes

---

## The Calculation That Broke Physics

Google just did something impossible.

**Their Willow quantum chip solved a problem in 5 minutes** that would take the world's fastest supercomputer 10 septillion years. That's 10,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000 years — longer than the universe has existed. [Google's Willow processor](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-024-07535-5) represents a significant breakthrough in quantum computing with its 105-qubit architecture and improved error correction.

---

## The Breakthrough Nobody Saw Coming

Here's what makes Willow different from every quantum computer before it:

**The game-changing stats:**
- **105 qubits** with [accuracy approaching 99.9%](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-024-07535-5)
- **5 minutes** to solve impossible problems
- **10^25 years** advantage over classical computers
- [Error correction](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-024-07535-5) that actually works, with logical error rates improving 2-5x from previous systems and scaling exponentially

> "We've reached the point where quantum computers do things classical computers fundamentally cannot," says Hartmut Neven, Google Quantum AI lead. "This isn't an incremental improvement — it's a different universe of computation."

**The killer feature**: Willow gets MORE accurate as you add qubits. Every previous quantum computer got worse. This changes everything.
