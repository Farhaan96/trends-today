# Samsung Galaxy S25 AI Revolution: The $14 Billion Partnership That Changes Smartphones Forever

**BREAKING:** Samsung just closed the most expensive AI deal in smartphone history. The Galaxy S25 series launches with Perplexity AI pre-installed, offering $200 worth of AI Pro features completely free to every user. This isn't just another phone upgrade—it's the beginning of truly intelligent mobile computing.

The partnership, part of Perplexity's massive $500 million funding round at a $14 billion valuation, positions Samsung as the first major smartphone manufacturer to embed advanced AI search directly into the core user experience. Starting January 22, 2025, every Galaxy S25 device becomes a portable AI powerhouse.

## The Game-Changing Partnership Details

Samsung's investment in Perplexity represents more than just financial backing—it's a complete reimagining of how smartphones access and process information. The deal includes:

- **Pre-installed Perplexity app** on all Galaxy S25 models (S25 at $799, S25 Plus at $999, S25 Ultra at $1,299)
- **Deep browser integration** with Perplexity's search capabilities replacing traditional search
- **Enhanced Bixby assistant** powered by Perplexity's reasoning engine
- **12 months of free Perplexity Pro** ($200 value) for all U.S. Galaxy device owners
- **Real-time AI research** capabilities for professional and academic use

"This partnership fundamentally changes what users can expect from their smartphone's intelligence," said Samsung's Mobile Experience division head. "We're not just adding another app—we're creating a new category of AI-native devices."
